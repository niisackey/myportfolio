About Me

Crafting Digital Experiences

I'm a dynamic and solutions-driven software developer with a strong foundation in Computer Science and growing impact across agriculture, finance, and data systems. I’ve built practical tools like AI-powered chatbots, data visualization systems, file optimizers, leave management systems, interactive dashboards, and waybill tracking platforms—always with a focus on solving real problems with clean, user-friendly tech. My passion for agriculture and data has led me to develop systems that pull, clean, and analyze large datasets from USDA sources—covering areas like crop insurance, risk management, and reinsurance reporting. I’m building tools that turn raw data into actionable insights for researchers, agribusiness stakeholders, and policymakers. But beyond code and crops… Trading the markets is one of my biggest passions. I’m currently building a custom forex system that pulls live market data, detects patterns, and provides intelligent trade signals. It's designed to help both new and experienced traders make smarter, data-driven decisions—and it's powered by the same principles I bring to all my projects: automation, precision, and simplicity. I work across technologies like Django, Flask, and Excel VBA, with skills in database design, cloud deployment, and offline-first applications. Whether I’m coding logistics tools or decoding candlestick patterns, I love creating systems that work hard in the background so people don’t have to. I'm committed to using technology to make an impact—across agriculture, logistics, finance, and beyond. Always learning, always building.
